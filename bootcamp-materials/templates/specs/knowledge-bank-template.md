## Template Usage Notes

**How to Use This Template:**

1. **Create a copy** for each feature in `specs/[feature-name]/knowledge_bank.md`
2. **Update regularly** - ideally after every work session
3. **Be detailed** in decision rationale (your future self will thank you)
4. **Capture failures** as well as successes (they're learning opportunities)
5. **Link liberally** to related documents and code
6. **Review periodically** to extract patterns and insights

**Who Uses This:**
- Current team: Track progress and decisions
- Future team: Understand why things are the way they are
- New team members: Get up to speed quickly
- Stakeholders: Stay informed on progress
